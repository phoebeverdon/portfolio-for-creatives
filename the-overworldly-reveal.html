The Overworldly Reveal

Most people were fast asleep on the trip to New Delhi except for one little girl, Stacy. She couldn’t fall asleep, no matter how hard she tried. Fortunately for her, sleep was no longer a problem. In the dark of the night, a blue light shone bright. Those fast asleep would’ve missed the magical phenomenon of a blue dragon, Satpal, who resembled India’s national bird: the peacock.

For this project, I illustrated an image pair connected by a narrative. I chose to do a conceal and reveal scenario, with the choice of flat colours and minimal rendering for a cartoon look. Satpal, the dragon, is native to India and is inspired by its legends and animals.
