Unvieling Eclipse

A teenager, Blessica, realizes she possesses both Sun and Moon powers— a phenomenon that has never occurred before, changing the course of politics forever.

The Sun and Moon powers were inspired by the elements the respective planets control: fire for the Sun and Water for the moon.

Blessica’s culture is inspired by the Philippines, specifically during the colonial Spanish period. To reflect this, she’s wearing a dress ensemble inspired by what women wore during that time, the Balintawak. Her dress is in cool colours to reflect she’s part of the Moon nation.
