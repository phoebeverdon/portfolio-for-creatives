The Fish Delivery Gnome

I created character and prop design for a potential 3D animated original film, the Fish Delivery Gnome.

I did a turnaround, expression sheet, and props designs for Fishel, the main character of the project.

In addition, I explored the expressions, colour palette, and props designs for Mira, the supporting character.
